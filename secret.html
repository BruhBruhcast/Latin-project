Item #: SCP-001-QIU
Object Class: Apollyon Keter
Special Containment Procedures: Due to its size, SCP-001-QIU cannot be contained. The present incarnation of SCP-001-QIU and its 'family' is to be monitored at all times. The anomaly's place of living and place of education should be continually monitored and if necessary, destroyed with a 1-kiloton nuke hidden on the grounds. The anomaly's dietary, mental and physical habits should be monitored and changed to make sure it stays obese and of below-average intelligence. To prevent the anomaly developing knowledge on its true identity, it is to be given 1 Class A Amnestic every month. Should this change, the Administrator and O5 Council must be contacted immediately and Procedure ZMAK is to be commenced, as this may constitute the beginning of a XK-Class End of the World Scenario
(edited ##/##/####) Due to the discovery of SCP-001-QIU-J, SCP-001-QIU is to be kept under greater surveillance and a kiloton nuke is to always be ready to commence Procedure ZMAK. SCP-001-QIU-J is to be under the same 'surveillance-by-nuke' as SCP-001-QIU.
